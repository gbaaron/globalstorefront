Privacy Policy

Global Storefront — Mobile App & Web Platform

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Global Storefront ("we", "us", "our") operates the Global Storefront mobile application and web platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When store owners create an account, we collect their name, email address, username, and company name.

Customer Chat Data: When customers interact with a store's chatbot widget, we collect the messages exchanged during the conversation and an optional name if provided.

Device Information: When using the mobile app, we collect device tokens for push notification delivery. We also collect the device platform (iOS or Android).

Usage Data: We collect basic usage information such as login timestamps to maintain account security.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the core service: enabling store owners to manage customer conversations
  • To deliver push notifications when customers need assistance
  • To power AI-assisted chatbot responses using store knowledge bases
  • To maintain account security and prevent unauthorized access

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI — Powers the AI chatbot. Customer messages are sent to OpenAI's API for response generation. OpenAI's usage policies apply.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — Delivers push notifications to mobile devices.
  • Airtable — Stores account data, conversations, and knowledge base content.
  • Netlify — Hosts the web application and serverless functions.

4. Data Retention

Conversation data is retained for as long as the store owner's account is active. Store owners can resolve and archive conversations at any time. Account deletion requests can be made by contacting us directly.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted passwords using bcrypt hashing
  • JWT-based authentication with expiring tokens
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Server-side API key storage (never exposed to clients)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for business owners and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
